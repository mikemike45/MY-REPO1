LIST OF INGEREDIENTS

  1. Flour

  2. Nutella

  3. Egg

Steps:

    Put flour Inside Bowl

  • Crack 3 eggs

  • pour 250g of Nutella in the bowl

  • Mix it

  • Pour the mixture into a baking pan

  • Bake at 175 degrees celsius for 15 minutes

  • Enjoy!
